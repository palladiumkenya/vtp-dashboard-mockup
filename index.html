Welcome

Since the inception of the National Data Warehouse (NDWH), a cumulative total of 2695 health facilities across 45 counties in Kenya, supported by various service delivery partners (SDPs), have uploaded care and treatment data to the platform.

As of May 2026, 1821 facilities submitted care and treatment datasets to the NDWH, supported by 34 SDPs operating in 39 counties.
